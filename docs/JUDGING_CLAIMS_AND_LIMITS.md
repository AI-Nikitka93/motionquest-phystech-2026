# MotionQuest Judging Claims And Limits

Date: 2026-05-06
Applies to: app copy, README, Devpost abstract, presentation, evidence surface and Q&A.

## Claims MotionQuest Will Prove

| Claim | Exact wording | Evidence needed in product |
|---|---|---|
| Accessibility | MotionQuest supports physical-activity practice without wearables, accounts, backend setup or specialized hardware. | Browser-only app, webcam flow, large UI, privacy note, no-login start. |
| Research alignment | MotionQuest maps short game-like tasks to evidence-inspired movement constructs. | Chair-stand framing, reach-task framing, tracking confidence, research panel. |
| Caregiver-readable outcome | MotionQuest turns one movement session into plain-language observations and limitations. | Report with reps, targets, confidence, interpretation, method and disclaimer. |
| Seated inclusion | MotionQuest treats seated participation as a first-class path, not as failed setup. | Ability choice, seated branch, hand-based seated tracking, report mode label. |
| Dignity and privacy | MotionQuest makes camera control, no account and no video upload visible before movement. | Welcome/privacy card, no-backend implementation, no-video-storage statement. |
| Confidence honesty | MotionQuest reports whether a session is live, sample, usable or limited-confidence. | Report source label, tracking validity, confidence-by-mode and limitations. |

## Claims MotionQuest Must Not Make

| Forbidden claim | Banned wording | Safe replacement |
|---|---|---|
| Medical diagnosis | diagnoses frailty, impairment, injury, disease or rehabilitation status | supports movement practice and communication |
| Fall prediction | predicts fall risk or prevents falls | shows session-level movement observations |
| Clinical validation | clinically validated, medical-grade, equivalent to in-person assessment | research-aligned hackathon prototype |
| Business proof | proven market, guaranteed revenue, paid demand, enterprise-ready | plausible caregiver/community wellness path without fake numbers |
| Accuracy proof | accurate body measurement, perfect webcam tracking, validated pose score | visible session observation with confidence and limitations |

## Required Disclaimer

MotionQuest is a hackathon prototype for physical-activity practice and communication. It is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, fall-risk predictor or substitute for professional evaluation.

## Confidence By Mode

| Mode | What Can Be Claimed | What Cannot Be Claimed | Required Report Language |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standing Adaptive Chair Movement | Visible sit-to-stand practice signal when enough body landmarks are visible. | Official chair-stand score, lower-body strength diagnosis, fall-risk inference. | "Standing movement was observed as a practice signal, not a clinical score." |
| Seated Adaptive Movement | Seated hand/upper-body participation signal when at least one open hand is visible. | Sitting diagnosis, wheelchair assessment, lower-body movement score. | "Seated movement used visible hand participation; lower-body observation is not required for this path." |
| Reach Stars | Visible hand-to-target interaction and dwell-based game hit signal. | Medical range-of-motion assessment or balance score. | "Reach Stars is a reach-practice proxy, not a clinical measurement." |
| Low-confidence live session | Camera attempted live observation but visibility/quality was insufficient. | Valid activity score. | "Session not valid enough; repeat with better camera framing before using numbers." |
| Safe sample session | Demonstrates report format and fallback flow. | Live camera measurement. | "Safe demo data only; not live camera data." |

## Live / Sample Proof Boundaries

| Source | Allowed Use | Required Visible Label | Forbidden Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Live camera session | Show actual app behavior, tracking readiness and report generation. | `Source: live camera session` plus confidence/validity. | Hiding low-confidence state or treating invalid numbers as usable. |
| Safe demo fallback | Demonstrate report shape when camera/stage conditions fail. | Persistent sample/fallback language in report and export. | Presenting sample numbers as live measurement. |
| Screenshots/video | Backup proof for presentation continuity. | File/source context and date where practical. | Claiming screenshots prove real-world clinical accuracy. |

## Numeric Evidence Rule

No numeric landmark-loss, detection accuracy, tracking reliability, conversion, market-size or business-performance claim may appear in public copy unless the exact number is tied to one of these:

- a named cited source next to the claim;
- a MotionQuest validation artifact with date, setup and evidence;
- an explicitly labeled measured result with method and scope.

Safe default wording is qualitative: `limited`, `confidence-aware`, `visible enough`, `not valid enough`, `sample only`, `practice signal`.
