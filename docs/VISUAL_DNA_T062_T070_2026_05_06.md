## T068 Unique Visual DNA

MotionQuest visual DNA:

- Warm kinetic trust.
- Dignity-first movement lab.
- Caregiver-readable evidence artifact.
- Camera as a bounded measurement stage, not a surveillance feed.
- Seated and standing users shown with equal dignity.
- Research-informed without becoming clinical.
- Game-like feedback without turning the product into a toy.

Forbidden drift:

- Hospital dashboard.
- Generic SaaS gradient landing.
- Childish arcade score chase.
- Raw AI pose demo.
- Medical diagnosis or fall-risk prediction interface.

## T069 Typography, Spacing, Density And Affordance Closure

Closure rules now documented and covered by tests:

- Minimum text is 16px.
- Default body copy is 18-20px or larger depending on surface.
- Primary controls are 56-64px tall.
- Critical actions have text labels.
- Focus rings are visible.
- Cards stay at 8px radius and buttons at 12px radius.
- Dense report content is separated into observed activity, confidence, limitations, interpretation, next step and export.

Existing automated checks:

- `visible interactive controls and text respect visual accessibility floor`
- `key visual sections keep accessible contrast`
- `keyboard flow and focus rings cover primary actions`
- `reduced motion keeps navigation usable without animated emphasis`
