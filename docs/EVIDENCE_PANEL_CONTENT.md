## Panel

Title:
Why MotionQuest is research-aligned

Body:
MotionQuest uses short webcam-guided activities inspired by older-adult exergame, chair-based activity and functional-movement research. The product focuses on session signals a browser can honestly observe: visible practice events, reach target interactions, timing when available and tracking confidence. It is designed for movement practice and caregiver communication, not diagnosis.

Bullets:
- Exergame research with older adults studies balance, functional performance, motivation and engagement.
- AI exercise tools often use sensing, tracking and real-time feedback.
- Chair-stand and reach-style activities are useful practice inspirations, but MotionQuest does not reproduce clinical scoring or range-of-motion assessment.
- Dignity and privacy are part of the method: camera use is session-bound and video is not saved.
- Confidence-by-mode explains what the app could and could not observe.

Footer:
Research-aligned prototype. Not a medical device.
